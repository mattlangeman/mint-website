Chris Gillespie is a Climate and Energy Policy Advisor with 15 years of experience supporting government decision making at all levels. Chris has led and contributed to the development of multiple infrastructure resilience planning guides for federal agencies, as well as climate resilience and action plans at state and local levels. Chris is also an expert in energy systems, regulation, and greenhouse gas emissions accounting. 

Chris is passionate about helping communities plan and respond to the emerging challenges of the future, primarily related to climate change and infrastructure systems planning, but also including resource constraints, evolving technologies, and policy implementation barriers.
